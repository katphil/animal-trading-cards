Common Bottlenose Dolphin

When the common bottlenose dolphin sleeps, one side of its brain stays awake. This allows the animal to continue swimming and coming to the surface to breathe.

  • Scientific Name: Tursiops truncatus
  • Average Length: 6.6 and 13.1 ft
  • Average Lifespan: 17 years
  • Habitat: Temperate and tropical waters

The TV Show "Flipper" brought bottlenose dolphins into the public conscienceness. The common bottlenose dolphin is not endangered at all, and is also not the same as mahi-mahi, the Hawaiian word for dolphin.
